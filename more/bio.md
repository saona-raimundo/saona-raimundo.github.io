# Bio (for talks)

Rai is a Fellow at London School of Economics (LSE). His research involves Mathematics, Game Theory, and Computer Science. He works with continuous and discrete models, and with both dynamic online and static offline settings. Before LSE, he completed his PhD at Institute of Science and Technology Austria (ISTA) under the supervision of Krishnendu Chatterjee.
